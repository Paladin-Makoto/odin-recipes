Lasagna

Layers of pasta sauce of beef and aromatics, sandwiched between layers flat pasta.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Water

Directions

  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Add Spaghetti Sauce
  3. Combine with Cheese and Eggs, Set aside
  4. Cook Pasta, drain
  5. Assemble Lasagna
  6. Bake covered, for 45 mins
  7. Uncover and bake for 10 more mins
  8. Serve

Details and Timing

  • Prep Time: 10 Mins
  • Cook Time: 70 Mins
  • Addt Time: 10 Mins
  • Total Time: 90 Mins
  • Serves: 12 pax
